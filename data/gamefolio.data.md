# Game Portfolio

Games published on itch.io under the `danio.quero` account. Source: https://danioquero.itch.io/

## Family Zoo - Interactive Map

- A UI-driven Android app experiment featuring an interactive map of an imaginary zoo, with tappable Points of Interest that display info about animal exhibitions.
- Showcases Unity UI design applied to an educational, non-game context.
- Link: https://danioquero.itch.io/family-zoo

## Theseus and the Minotaur?

- A 3-level UI-driven puzzle remake where you guide a no-poly Theseus through maze levels while evading a relentless (but dim) Minotaur.
- Available for Windows, controlled with WASD or arrow keys.
- Link: https://danioquero.itch.io/theseus-minotaur

## Dungeon Escape - The Demo

- An Android dungeon-crawl demo where you run, jump, and fight through monster-filled dungeons collecting diamonds.
- Diamonds can be spent in an in-game shop to power up your character or buy the key to the next level.
- Link: https://danioquero.itch.io/dungeon-escape

## The Great Fleece by Danio

- A short stealth-adventure game for Windows where you guide Darren across a room full of guards and security cameras to reach the vault.
- Supports both point-and-click and keyboard input.
- Link: https://danioquero.itch.io/the-great-fleece

## Galaxy Shooter 2D

- A Galaga-style arcade space shooter for Windows spanning 3 levels, with multiple enemy types, temporary power-ups, and a boost mechanic.
- The developer's first published game, also used as the basis for a series of Unity tutorials on Medium.
- Link: https://danioquero.itch.io/galaxy-shooter-2d
